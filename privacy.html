Privacy Policy

FreeTimeline is designed so your timeline data stays on your device.

What is stored

  • Timeline events, people, characters, categories, settings, and uploaded images may be saved in your browser storage.
  • The cookie notice preference is also saved locally.
  • No advertising cookies are used by these static pages.

Your control

You can export JSON backups from each tool, delete browser storage, or use the Blank Timeline action inside a tool.
