Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

There are also similar technologies such as localStorage and sessionStorage, which serve similar purposes but work slightly differently.

How FreeTimeline Uses Browser Storage

FreeTimeline is designed with privacy as a core principle. We primarily use localStorage rather than traditional cookies. Here's what we store and why:

Storage Key Purpose Type Duration
inf_universe_v4 Stores your Universe Timeline data (events, characters, etc.) localStorage Until you clear browser data
inf_biography_v1 Stores your Biography Timeline data (milestones, memories) localStorage Until you clear browser data
ft_consent_acknowledged Remembers that you've seen our cookie/storage notice localStorage Until you clear browser data

Important: Your Data Never Leaves Your Device

All data stored in localStorage remains on your device. FreeTimeline does not transmit, sync, or backup this data to any server. We cannot access, read, or recover your data because we never receive it in the first place.

Third-Party Cookies and Advertising

When you visit FreeTimeline, you may see advertisements served by third-party advertising networks. These networks may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Display advertisements that are relevant to you based on your browsing history
  • Limit the number of times you see a particular advertisement
  • Measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns
  • Understand your interests based on websites you have visited

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies, including the DoubleClick cookie, to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the internet. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to FreeTimeline and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices.

Note About Blocking Cookies

If you block localStorage completely, FreeTimeline's core functionality (saving your timeline data) will not work. We recommend allowing localStorage for freetimeline.pt while managing third-party advertising cookies according to your preferences.
