Roadmap

An honest, public list of what we're building. We ship slowly and deliberately — no breaking changes, no surprise paywalls. Got a request? Tell us.

Recently shipped Done

  • Local-first storage for both Universe and Biography Everything stays in your browser — no accounts, no servers.
  • JSON export & import Take your data with you, back it up, move between devices.
  • Guides section Articles for novelists, GMs, journalers, and world-builders.

In progress Building

  • Improved mobile timeline view A smoother, touch-first experience for phones and tablets.
  • Richer event editor Markdown notes, image attachments stored locally, better tagging.
  • Refreshed Contact & About pages Direct email, clearer expectations, public roadmap (this page).

Planned Next

  • Multi-timeline workspace Switch between several Universes or Biographies without exporting.
  • Character & relationship graph view See how people, factions, and arcs connect across time.
  • Encrypted local backup files Optional password-protected exports for sensitive journals.
  • Print & PDF export Beautiful, shareable printouts of any timeline range.
  • Community templates Starter packs for popular fictional universes and journaling formats.
  • Optional Discord community A place for writers, GMs, and memory keepers to swap tips.
  • Accessibility pass Full keyboard navigation, screen-reader labels, reduced-motion mode.
  • Localised UI Portuguese first, then Spanish, French, and German.

This roadmap is a direction, not a promise. Priorities may shift based on what we learn from you. If a feature here matters to you, let us know — it really does move things up the list.
